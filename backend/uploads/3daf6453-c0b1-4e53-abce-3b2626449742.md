# Acme Corp. Remote Work Policy (2026)

## Eligibility
All employees who have completed 90 days of service are eligible to apply for remote work.
Eligible roles include: engineering, design, marketing, and customer support.
Manufacturing and warehouse roles are excluded because of physical-presence requirements.

## Reimbursements
Approved remote employees receive a one-time home-office stipend of $1,200
plus a monthly internet reimbursement of $75. Receipts must be submitted within 30 days
of purchase. The stipend is payable on the 15th of the month following approval.

## Security
Remote workers must use the company VPN (GlobalProtect) whenever handling
confidential customer data. Personal devices are not permitted. Company-issued
MacBook Pro 14 devices are the only approved hardware.

## Working Hours
Core collaboration hours are 10:00–16:00 local time. Outside of these hours,
employees may set their own schedule. Employees must be reachable via Slack
during core hours unless on PTO or sick leave.

## Revocation
The remote work arrangement may be revoked at any time if performance drops
below expectations for two consecutive quarters.
